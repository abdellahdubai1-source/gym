Understanding BMI

< 18.5

Underweight — a coach can help you build lean mass safely.

18.5 – 24.9

Healthy range — maintain with consistent training and nutrition.

25 – 29.9

Overweight — resistance training plus a structured diet plan helps most.

30+

Obese — a personalised program with medical guidance is recommended.

BMI is a general screening measure and does not account for muscle mass, bone density or body composition. Speak with one of our trainers for a full assessment.
