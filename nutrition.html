Hydration

Hydration Tips

  • • Aim for 35ml of water per kg of bodyweight daily as a baseline.
  • • Add 500ml for every 45 minutes of intense training.
  • • Include electrolytes on high-sweat days, especially in Dubai's heat.
  • • Check urine colour — pale yellow is the target.
Supplements

Supplement Guide

  • • Creatine monohydrate — 5g daily, the most evidence-backed strength supplement.
  • • Whey or plant protein — to help close daily protein gaps.
  • • Vitamin D3 — commonly low in indoor-trained athletes.
  • • Always check with our nutritionist before adding anything new.
