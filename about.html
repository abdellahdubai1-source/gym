About Iron Pulse

Fifteen Years
Of Discipline

Our Story

Founded On The Gym Floor,
Not In A Boardroom

Iron Pulse Fitness opened in 2011 with one rented floor and a handful of barbells. Founder and former powerlifter Rashid Al Nuaimi built the club on a simple belief: that world-class coaching, not fancy branding, is what actually changes bodies.

Fifteen years later, that belief hasn't changed. We've added floors, machines and specialists, but every program is still written by a coach who knows your name, your history and your goal.
